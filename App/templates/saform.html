Yelp Review Classification Example

Introduction

The example is based on Yelp business review dataset. Hashing vectorizer and Multi-class Logistic Regression is used for this model example. After you write down your own review and submit, you would get predicted label from 1 star to 5 star and corresponding probability for each label. Please submit your true review label after checking the result, which would help the model to be more accurate.

Review Example: The food wasn't that great and the service was okay. We should have just gone. The steak and eggs had no flavor and the potatoes were room temperature. I guess this is what happens when you want breakfast for dinner. The bill was $46 which is fine but if the food was actually good I don't think I would be annoyed.
